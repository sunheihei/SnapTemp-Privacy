Privacy Policy

SnapTemp is designed to help you capture temporary photos and videos while keeping your photo library clean. We aim to keep your data on your device whenever possible.

Last updated: May 15, 2026

Chinese translation is available below.

1. Information We Collect

SnapTemp does not actively collect, upload, sell, or share your photos, videos, QR code content, recognized text, or photo library content. The current version does not include advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, or third-party crash reporting SDKs.

The app stores information locally on your device to provide its features, including:

  • Photos and videos captured in the app, thumbnails, creation dates, expiration dates, and related local records.
  • App settings such as retention period, watermark settings, quality settings, expiration reminders, and blur badge preferences.
  • Photo organization state, such as whether a month has already been organized, to improve the organization workflow.

2. Permissions

SnapTemp requests system permissions only when they are needed for related features.

  • Camera: Used to take photos, record videos, and detect QR codes in the camera preview.
  • Microphone: Used only when recording videos with audio.
  • Photo Library Read Access: Used to browse and organize your system photo library, including organizing by month, moving items into albums, or moving items to trash.
  • Photo Library Add Access: Used to save photos or videos you choose to your system photo library.
  • Notifications: Used to remind you when temporary files are about to expire. You can disable notifications in system settings at any time.

3. Photos, Videos, and Recognition Results

Photos and videos captured in SnapTemp are stored locally in the app by default and are not automatically uploaded to a server. Unless you choose to save them to your system photo library, they will not be written to Photos.

Text recognition and QR code recognition are used to help you view image content or open QR code links. Recognition results are processed and displayed locally on your device. SnapTemp does not send these results to us.

4. Auto Cleanup and Deletion

SnapTemp can automatically clean up temporary files based on the retention period you choose. Deletion happens locally on your device. For items in your system photo library, the app uses system photo library capabilities only when you explicitly choose to organize, move, or delete items.

5. VIP and Purchases

SnapTemp may offer VIP features such as longer retention periods, custom watermarks, or an ad-free experience. In the current app, VIP status is stored locally in device settings.

If formal purchases are offered through the App Store in the future, payment and refund processing will be handled by Apple. SnapTemp does not directly receive or store your card number, payment account, or full payment details.

6. Third-Party Services

The current version does not include advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, or cloud sync services. If third-party services are added in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy and update the app privacy information in App Store Connect.

7. Data Security

Because SnapTemp mainly processes data locally on your device, protecting your device is important. We recommend using a device passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID, and carefully managing photo library and notification permissions.

8. Children’s Privacy

SnapTemp does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us using the information below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy because of feature changes, legal requirements, or review requirements. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new date at the top.

隐私政策

SnapTemp 的设计目标是让你放心拍摄、临时保存和整理照片。我们尽量把数据留在你的设备本地。

最后更新：2026 年 5 月 15 日

1. 我们收集哪些信息

SnapTemp 不会主动收集、上传或出售你的照片、视频、二维码内容、识别文字或相册内容。当前版本没有集成广告 SDK、第三方统计 SDK 或第三方崩溃分析 SDK。

App 会在你的设备本地保存必要的信息，用于实现基础功能，例如：

  • 你拍摄的照片和视频文件，以及它们的缩略图、创建时间、过期时间等本地记录。
  • 你的 App 设置，例如保留时长、水印设置、画质设置、是否开启过期提醒、是否隐藏模糊照片等。
  • 系统相册整理状态，例如某个月份是否已经整理过。这些信息用于改善整理体验。

2. 权限使用说明

SnapTemp 只在实现相关功能时请求系统权限。

  • 相机权限：用于拍摄照片、录制视频，以及在相机预览中识别二维码。
  • 麦克风权限：仅在录制视频并需要录入声音时使用。
  • 相册读取权限：用于浏览系统相册、按月份整理照片、将照片移动到相册或移入垃圾桶。
  • 相册写入权限：用于把你选择保存的照片或视频写入系统相册。
  • 通知权限：用于提醒你临时文件即将过期。你可以随时在系统设置中关闭通知。

3. 照片、视频和识别内容

你在 SnapTemp 中拍摄的照片和视频默认保存在 App 本地，不会自动上传到服务器。除非你主动选择保存到系统相册，否则它们不会被写入系统相册。

文字识别和二维码识别用于帮助你查看图片内容或打开二维码链接。识别结果仅在设备本地处理和显示，SnapTemp 不会把识别结果发送给我们。

4. 自动清理和删除

SnapTemp 支持按你设置的保留时长自动清理临时文件。删除操作会发生在你的设备本地。对于系统相册中的照片，App 只会在你明确执行整理、移动或删除操作时调用系统相册能力。

5. VIP 和购买

SnapTemp 可能提供 VIP 功能，例如更长的保留时长、自定义水印或无广告体验。当前 App 中的 VIP 状态保存在本地设备设置中。

如果未来通过 App Store 提供正式购买，支付和退款流程将由 Apple 处理。SnapTemp 不会直接接收或存储你的银行卡号、支付账号或完整付款信息。

6. 第三方服务

当前版本未集成广告 SDK、第三方分析 SDK 或云同步服务。若未来加入第三方服务，我们会更新本隐私政策，并在 App Store Connect 中同步更新 App 隐私信息。

7. 数据安全

由于 SnapTemp 主要在设备本地处理数据，保护设备本身也很重要。请使用系统锁屏密码、Face ID 或 Touch ID，并谨慎管理相册和通知权限。

8. 儿童隐私

SnapTemp 不面向儿童主动收集个人信息。如果你认为儿童向我们提供了个人信息，请通过下方联系方式联系我们。
